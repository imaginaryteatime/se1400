Website Summary

What Will the site contain?

The site will contain tips and tricks for different aspects of costume including wig styling, sewing tips, where to buy costumes, and convention information

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will souce photos from the internet, my own photos, and other people in the community.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Not everyone will, but people looking for the specific information will appreciate having many different resources in one place.

Why should a site be built for this?

For people only just getting into the hobby, it can be hard to find information. Having a website that links other sites and resources can make is a lot easier for someone wanting to participate to start.

What would you like users to do at your site?

It's supposed to be more of a learning website than anything. It's main purpose is to connect people to information easier so they can start a new hobby.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of this site is to inform and connect people about costume making.
